A protein-packed salad with juicy grilled chicken, crisp veggies, and a tangy homemade dressing.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cups chopped romaine lettuce
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 grilled corn cob (kernels removed)

Dressing

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper for 30 minutes.
  2. Grill chicken over medium-high heat for 5‚Äì6 minutes per side until cooked through.
  3. Let chicken rest, then slice into strips.
  4. In a bowl, combine lettuce, tomatoes, onion, avocado, and corn.
  5. Top with grilled chicken and drizzle with dressing.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
